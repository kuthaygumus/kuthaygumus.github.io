Bebito

Sleep, Feeding & Diaper Log

Track Everything

One app for every part of your baby's day.

  • Feeding — breast, bottle & solids
  • Sleep — naps and night sleep
  • Diapers — wet, dirty & mixed
  • Growth — weight, height & head
  • Milestones — firsts and memories
  • Health — vaccines, meds & visits
  • Parent Mood — how you're doing

Features

Designed for tired parents who need things to just work.

Smart Insights
Patterns and predictions based on your baby's data.
Apple Watch
Quick logging right from your wrist, even one-handed.
Widgets & Live Activities
See timers and last events on your Home Screen and Dynamic Island.
Data Export
Export to CSV or PDF for your pediatrician or your records.
Multi-Baby Support
Track multiple children, each with their own profile.
Caregiver Sharing
Share access with partners, grandparents, or nannies.
Siri Shortcuts
Log events hands-free with custom voice commands.
