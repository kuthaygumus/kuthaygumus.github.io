Getting Started

Welcome to Bebito! To get started, open the app and tap Add Baby to create your first baby profile. Enter your baby's name, birth date, and optionally a photo. Once your profile is set up, you can start logging feedings, sleep, diapers, and more from the main screen.

Each event type has a quick-log button for fast entry. Tap and hold for more detailed options like notes, photos, or specific measurements.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track feedings?

Tap the Feeding button on the main screen. Choose the type: breastfeeding (with side and duration), bottle (with amount), or solids (with food description). Bebito tracks timing, amounts, and patterns to help you see your baby's feeding rhythm over time.

How do I use the sleep timer?

Tap Sleep and then Start when your baby falls asleep. Tap Stop when they wake up. You can also add sleep entries manually if you forgot to start the timer. Bebito tracks total sleep, nap counts, and shows sleep patterns in your daily and weekly summaries.

How do I log diaper changes?

Tap Diaper and select the type: wet, dirty, or mixed. You can optionally add notes or a photo. Bebito tracks diaper counts by type to help you monitor your baby's output, which is especially useful for newborns.

How do I track growth and milestones?

Go to your baby's profile to log Growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference). Bebito plots these on growth charts so you can see progress over time. For Milestones, tap the milestones section to record firsts and special moments with dates and photos.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data. Choose between CSV (for spreadsheets) or PDF (for sharing with your pediatrician). You can select a date range and which event types to include. Exports are generated on your device and can be shared via any method available on your iPhone.

How do I use widgets and Live Activities?

Long-press your Home Screen, tap the + button, and search for Bebito. Choose from available widgets to see last feeding time, sleep status, or daily summaries at a glance. Live Activities appear on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island during active timers like sleep or feeding sessions.

How do I set up Apple Watch?

Install the Bebito Apple Watch app from the Watch app on your iPhone. Once installed, you can quickly log feedings, diapers, and start sleep timers directly from your wrist. Data syncs automatically with your iPhone.

What do I get with Bebito Pro?

Bebito Pro unlocks smart insights and predictions, iCloud sync across devices, data export (CSV and PDF), multi-baby support, caregiver sharing, Apple Watch app, widgets, Live Activities, Siri Shortcuts, and detailed statistics. Free users get basic tracking for one baby.

Managing Subscriptions

All subscriptions are managed through Apple. To view, change, or cancel your subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Bebito to manage your plan

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. For refunds, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
