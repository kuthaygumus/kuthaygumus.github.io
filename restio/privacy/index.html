Restio is a mindful rest timer designed to help you build healthier screen break habits. Your privacy is fundamental to how we build Restio. This policy explains what data we access, how it is handled, and the choices you have.

🚫

Data We Do Not Collect

Restio does not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data collection services. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information to anyone — ever.

In short: No tracking. No ads. No data brokers. Your rest habits are yours alone.

📱

On-Device Data

All your rest sessions, streaks, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to enable iCloud sync.

☁️

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, Restio uses your personal iCloud account to sync data across your Apple devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is accessible only to you. We cannot access your iCloud data. Apple's iCloud privacy practices govern this storage.

👥

CloudKit & Buddy Feature

If you choose to use the buddy feature, Restio uses Apple CloudKit to share limited information with your selected buddy:

  • Your display name (chosen by you)
  • Your current rest streak

This sharing is entirely user-initiated and can be stopped at any time by removing your buddy connection. No other personal data is shared through this feature.

❤️

Apple HealthKit

With your explicit permission, Restio can write Mindful Session data to Apple Health. This is write-only access — Restio does not read any health data from HealthKit.

HealthKit data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers or any external service. Restio does not use HealthKit data for advertising or data mining purposes, in full compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

📷

Camera Access

Restio may request camera access for on-device face detection to enhance the rest experience. All processing happens locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework.

Important: No images or video are ever stored, transmitted, recorded, or sent off your device. Camera data is processed in real time and immediately discarded.

💳

Subscriptions

Restio offers optional premium subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment details, billing address, or Apple ID credentials. All purchase transactions are governed by Apple's privacy practices.

🧒

Children's Privacy

Restio does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The app does not require account creation, login, or personal data input of any kind.

📝

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

✉️
