Privacy Policy

Restio ("we," "us," or "the app") is a mindful rest timer for iOS designed to help you take healthier screen breaks. This Privacy Policy describes what information Restio accesses, how that information is handled, and the choices available to you.

Information We Do Not Collect

Restio does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to any server owned or operated by us. The app contains no analytics frameworks, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporting services, and no third-party tracking of any kind. We do not sell, share, or rent user data to any third party.

On-Device Data

Your rest sessions, streaks, preferences, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple frameworks. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us unless you choose to enable iCloud sync as described below.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, Restio may sync your app data across your Apple devices through your private iCloud container. This data is accessible only to you through your Apple ID. We do not have the ability to access, read, or modify your iCloud data. Apple's Privacy Policy governs iCloud storage and transmission.

CloudKit and Buddy Feature

Restio offers an optional buddy feature that allows you to share limited information with another user you select. If you choose to use this feature, the following data is shared via Apple CloudKit:

  • A display name you provide
  • Your current rest streak count

This sharing is initiated solely by you and may be discontinued at any time by removing the buddy connection. No additional personal data is transmitted through this feature. CloudKit data is processed through Apple's infrastructure and is governed by Apple's privacy practices. Data shared through the buddy feature may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence in accordance with Apple's data handling practices.

Apple HealthKit

With your explicit prior consent, Restio may write Mindful Session entries to Apple HealthKit. This access is write-only — Restio does not read, query, or access any existing health data from HealthKit.

All HealthKit data remains on your device (or in your private Health data store if you use iCloud for Health). Restio does not use HealthKit data for advertising, data mining, or any purpose other than recording your rest sessions. This is in full compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

In-App Purchases

Restio offers optional premium features through subscriptions and a one-time purchase, managed entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not receive, process, or store your payment information, billing address, or Apple ID credentials. All transaction data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because Restio does not collect data on external servers, there is no user data for us to retain. All app data stored on your device can be deleted at any time by deleting the app. If you use iCloud sync, you may also remove synced data through your iCloud storage settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, as well as the right to restrict or object to certain processing. Because Restio does not collect personal data on external servers, these rights are effectively exercised through your device and iCloud settings. If you have questions about exercising your rights, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Restio does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age (or the applicable minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction). The app does not require account creation, login credentials, or personal data input of any kind. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of Restio after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
