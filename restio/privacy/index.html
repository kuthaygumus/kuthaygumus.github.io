Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Restio ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mindful rest timer designed to help you build healthier screen break habits. Your privacy is fundamental to how we build Restio. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is handled.

Data We Do Not Collect

Restio does not use analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data collection services. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information to anyone.

On-Device Data

All your rest sessions, streaks, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud sync.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, Restio uses your personal iCloud account to sync your data across your Apple devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is accessible only to you. Apple's iCloud privacy policy governs this storage.

CloudKit (Buddy Feature)

If you choose to use the buddy feature, Restio uses Apple CloudKit to share limited information with your selected buddy:

  • Your display name (chosen by you)
  • Your current rest streak

This sharing is user-initiated and can be stopped at any time. No other personal data is shared through the buddy feature.

Apple HealthKit

With your explicit permission, Restio can write Mindful Session data to Apple Health. This is write-only access — Restio does not read any health data. HealthKit data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to external servers.

Camera Access

Restio may request camera access for on-device face detection to enhance the rest experience. All camera processing happens locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or recorded.

Subscriptions

Restio offers optional subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions.

Children's Privacy

Restio does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app does not require account creation or personal data input.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with a new effective date.
