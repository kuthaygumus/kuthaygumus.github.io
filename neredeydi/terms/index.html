Description of Service

Neredeydi is an entertainment and educational application that provides geography guess games covering Turkish provinces, mountains, lakes, rivers, plains, and four world continents. The app includes a Daily Mystery, four game modes (classic, timed, reverse, learning), a home-screen and Lock Screen widget, a Siri shortcut, and optional Apple Game Center leaderboards and achievements. The app is available for iPhone and iPad.
