Getting Started

Neredeydi is a geography guess game. Each round shows a place — a Turkish province, mountain, lake, river, plain, or a country in one of four world regions — and asks you to drop a pin on the map where you think it is. The closer to the real spot, the higher the score.

  1. Open the app and tap a category card on the home screen.
  2. Choose a game mode (classic, timed, reverse, or learning).
  3. Drop your pin on the map and confirm. Repeat for the rest of the round.
  4. See your score and a fun fact for each place at the end.

Daily Mystery

Every day at midnight UTC, a new place is featured. You get up to three progressive hints (abstract → specific). Solve before using all hints to keep your score higher. Solved days extend your streak (🔥). The Lock Screen and home-screen widgets show the current streak and whether today is solved.

Daily Mystery is free for all users. The same place is featured for everyone on a given day, so your shareable emoji tile (🟩🟨🟧🟥) is comparable with friends.

Game Modes

Classic

The default mode. Drop a pin on the map. Score scales with distance.

Timed

Each round has a per-question timer. Faster correct answers earn a time bonus.

Reverse

Multiple-choice mode. The map shows a place; pick the matching name from four options.

Learning

No score, no penalty — tap "reveal answer" to study. Best for first-time exposure to a category.

Game Center

Sign in to Game Center (Settings » Game Center) to appear on global leaderboards and unlock achievements. Sign-in is optional; all single-player features work without it.

Widget and Siri

Add the Neredeydi widget to your home screen or Lock Screen for at-a-glance daily streaks. Tap the widget to jump straight into today's mystery.

Try saying "Hey Siri, günün gizemi" (TR) or "Hey Siri, daily mystery" (EN) to launch the daily mystery from anywhere.

Neredeydi Pro

Pro is a one-time, lifetime purchase that unlocks unlimited play across all categories and world regions. There is no subscription. The Provinces category and Daily Mystery remain free even without Pro; other categories allow one free game per day.

Restoring purchases on a new device

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore purchases." Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Refunds

Troubleshooting

Map is empty or won't load

Maps are bundled with the app and rendered offline. If the map appears blank, force-quit the app (swipe up from the bottom and flick Neredeydi away) and reopen it.

Daily Mystery hasn't reset

Daily Mystery resets at midnight UTC, not local midnight. If you played late and tomorrow's mystery hasn't appeared yet, the day boundary may not have rolled over. Check back after UTC midnight.

Widget shows yesterday's data

Widgets refresh on a system schedule. If your streak in the widget seems stale, opening the app once will trigger a fresh widget update.
