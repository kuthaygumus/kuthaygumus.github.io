Truce

A funny, private counter for the days since you last argued with your partner — usually stuck on Day 1. What day are YOU on?

It's not a fight tracker. It's a running joke.

You're not tracking your partner — you're laughing at how human we all are. When the streak breaks, Bicky the lovebird reacts, and you get a card worth screenshotting. Private, offline, no subscription.

The Counter
Days since your last argument — with your partner, your mom, your boss, or yourself. Mostly stuck on Day 1.
Bicky the Lovebird
Your deadpan, long-suffering witness. He reacts to every reset and quietly evolves the longer you keep him around.
The Reset Card
The funniest moment is the one you lose. Every reset auto-composes a share card worth screenshotting.
Truce Code Duel
Compare your day with a friend's — "your Day 1 vs their Day 9." No accounts, no sign-in, nothing leaves your phone.
Year in Fights
Your private history — longest peace, top reason, a harmony heatmap. The part a weekend clone can't copy: it's yours.
Widgets
Your current day right on the Home Screen and Lock Screen — a quiet little flex among your normal apps.
Private by Design
Everything stays on your device. Data Not Collected, no accounts, no ads, no tracking — plus an optional encrypted backup you control.
No Subscription, Ever
No renewals, no "Truce+", no surprise charges. The app is free, and everything extra is a one-time purchase.
