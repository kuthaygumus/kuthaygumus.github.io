9 Breathing Techniques

Each technique is paired with a unique plant in your garden.

Calm & Relaxation
  • 4-7-8 Breathing — a method for falling asleep and calming the mind
  • Coherent Breathing — heart-brain harmony for deep calm
  • Pursed Lip — gentle technique for stress relief
Focus & Energy
  • Box Breathing — used for mental clarity under pressure
  • Energizing 2-1-4 — a quick energy boost
  • Wim Hof Method — cold exposure breathing technique
Panic & Emergency
  • Panic Seed — quick breathing for moments of overwhelm
  • Alternate Nostril — helps balance your breathing rhythm
Sleep
  • Deep Calm — extended exhale for bedtime relaxation

Your Living Garden

Every breath plants a seed. Complete sessions to grow 9 unique plants from Seed to Full Bloom. Watch your garden grow as your practice develops.
